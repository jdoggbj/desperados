Homepage

Welcome to the home of Lewisville Baseball and Softball Association Select Baseball. LBSA is proud of the tradition we have built through it's Texas Desperados Select Teams. Established in 2010, the Texas Desperados have experienced many successes including Select League Winners, State Champions, World Series Winners, and many other tournament wins.
Please contact The Select Baseball Director, Josh Briscoe at 469 628 3582 or email jbriscoe46@gmail.com for more details on the LBA Select Baseball program
